# 🎤 Sentinex Demo Day Presentation

## 🚀 Slide 1: The Hook
- **Title**: Sentinex - Your Autonomous DeFi Guardian.
- **Problem**: 24/7 crypto markets are impossible for humans to monitor. Bots are either too simple or require giving up your private keys (Custodial).
- **Solution**: A 100% non-custodial AI Agent that trades based on real-time news sentiment, protected by Soroban smart contracts.

## 🧠 Slide 2: How it Works
- **Sentiment Analysis**: AI scans global news (CryptoPanic) using GPT-4.
- **The Guardian**: If sentiment turns extremely bullish/bearish, the AI triggers a trade.
- **Trustless Execution**: The AI *cannot* steal your funds. It only has "Swap" permissions. You keep your keys.
